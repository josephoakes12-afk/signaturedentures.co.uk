Cookies

Cookies on this site.

A simple overview of how cookies are used on our website.

Last updated: 8 February 2026

First-party cookies

This website does not set first-party analytics, advertising or preference cookies.

Our own site scripts do not write cookies, localStorage or sessionStorage.

Third-party services

Google Maps is embedded on the contact page and may set cookies when that page loads and when you interact with the map.

Formspree (our contact form processor) may use cookies or similar technical measures to deliver submissions and prevent spam or abuse.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies in your browser settings, including blocking or deleting them.

Blocking third-party cookies may affect embedded maps and contact form reliability.
