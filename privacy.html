Privacy

Privacy notice.

A clear summary of how we use your information when you contact us.

Last updated: 6 February 2026

What we collect

  • Name
  • Email
  • Telephone (if provided)
  • Message content
  • Any details you choose to include

How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries
  • To arrange consultations or appointments (if needed)
  • To provide ongoing support related to the enquiry

Form handling

Enquiry submissions are processed using Formspree (a form handling provider) and then delivered to Signature Dentures by email.

Formspree acts as a data processor for the purpose of handling form submissions.

Lawful basis & retention

We process your information to respond to your enquiry and provide our services.

We keep enquiries only as long as necessary to respond and for record-keeping.

Sharing & security

We do not sell your information. We only share it with service providers needed to run the site or handle enquiries (Formspree). If you would like details, please ask.

We take reasonable steps to protect your information.

Your rights & complaints

You can request access, correction, deletion, or object to processing. To exercise your rights, contact us at enquiries@signaturedentures.co.uk.

You can also complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).
