Privacy

Privacy notice.

A clear summary of how we use your information when you contact us.

Last updated: 8 February 2026

What we collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number (if provided)
  • Reason for enquiry and preferred contact method (if provided)
  • Message content
  • Technical submission data from Formspree (for example timestamp and IP address)

Please avoid including detailed medical information in the web form.

How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries
  • To review your request and provide the right next step
  • To contact you using your preferred method where provided
  • To maintain enquiry records and reduce spam/abuse

Form handling

The contact form sends your details to Formspree (our form processor), which forwards enquiries to Signature Dentures by email.

Formspree processes this data on our behalf to deliver submissions and provide spam protection.

Lawful basis & retention

We process enquiry information to take steps at your request and to manage our legitimate interests in handling enquiries.

We keep enquiries only as long as needed to respond, follow up and meet basic record-keeping requirements.

Sharing & security

We do not sell personal information. We share enquiry data only with providers needed to run the contact form and deliver responses, including Formspree.

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information.

Your rights & complaints

You can request access, correction, deletion, or object to processing. To exercise your rights, contact us at enquiries@signaturedentures.co.uk.

You can also complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).
