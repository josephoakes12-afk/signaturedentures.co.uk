What to expect

A calm, straightforward process with clear guidance at each step.

1. Initial consultation

We’ll discuss what you’re looking for, any previous denture concerns, and what would make the biggest difference to your comfort and confidence.

2. Assessment and impressions

We’ll assess your smile, facial profile and speech, then take impressions and measurements where needed to help achieve a natural appearance and stable fit.

3. Try-in and bite registration

Where appropriate, we’ll check the bite, tooth position and overall look before the final dentures are made. This step helps fine-tune comfort and aesthetics.

4. Fitting appointment

Your dentures are fitted and checked carefully. We’ll talk you through what to expect in the first few days and how to care for them.

5. Aftercare and adjustments

It’s common to need small adjustments as your mouth adapts. We’re here to help with comfort, fit and ongoing advice — so you feel confident wearing your dentures.

Everyone’s treatment is individual. We’ll confirm the most suitable approach and expected number of appointments after your consultation.
