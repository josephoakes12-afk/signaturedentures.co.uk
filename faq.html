Dentures, repairs and aftercare

Can my dentures be repaired?
Many breaks, cracks and loose teeth can be repaired professionally. We don’t recommend DIY repair kits or household glues as they can permanently damage the denture or affect the fit.
Can dentures be adjusted?
Yes. It’s common to need follow-up adjustments after fitting. Please don’t attempt to adjust dentures yourself.
Do dentures need replacing over time?
Over time, changes to the gums and jaw can affect how dentures fit. Some people may need a reline or replacement depending on comfort, stability and wear.
How do I clean my dentures?
Clean dentures daily using a denture-safe cleaner and a soft brush. Avoid boiling water, and follow product instructions. We’ll also advise on caring for gums and any remaining natural teeth.
For urgent repairs or comfort concerns, contact us as soon as you can and keep any broken pieces safe.
