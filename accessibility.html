Accessibility

Accessibility statement.

We want our website to be usable and clear for everyone.

Last updated: 8 February 2026

Our commitment

We aim to make this website usable for as many people as possible and to meet WCAG 2.2 AA standards where reasonably practical.

What we’ve improved

  • Skip link to main content and consistent page structure
  • Keyboard support for navigation, FAQ tabs and FAQ accordions
  • Visible focus states on links, buttons and form controls
  • Form labels, browser validation and aria-live status messaging
  • Reduced-motion support and responsive layouts

Known limitations

Embedded Google Maps content may not be fully accessible for all assistive technology users.

Third-party services (including maps and form processing) may have accessibility limitations outside our direct control.

We have not yet completed an independent third-party accessibility audit.

Accessibility help

If you need information in a different format or have trouble using the site, contact enquiries@signaturedentures.co.uk or call 07894 581463.

Please include the page URL and a brief description of the issue so we can investigate quickly.

This statement applies to https://signaturedentures.co.uk/
