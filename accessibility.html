Accessibility

Accessibility statement.

We want our website to be usable and clear for everyone.

Last updated: 6 February 2026

Our commitment

We aim to make our website accessible and usable for everyone. We aim to follow WCAG 2.2 AA where reasonably practical.

What we’ve improved

  • Clear headings and structure
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Visible focus states
  • Form labels and accessible error/validation messaging
  • Responsive layout for mobile

Known limitations

Embedded maps may not be fully accessible.

Third-party form handling may have limitations outside our control.

Accessibility help

If you need information in a different format or have trouble using the site, contact enquiries@signaturedentures.co.uk or call 07894581463.

This statement applies to https://signaturedentures.co.uk/
