About us

Family-run denture service in Cheadle, Stoke-on-Trent.

Signature Dentures is a family-run business focused on comfort, fit and personal care. We aim to provide clear guidance and a calm, professional experience from consultation through to fitting and aftercare.

Our team

Simon Owen

Clinical Dental Technician (CDT)

Simon Owen is a Clinical Dental Technician with over 20 years' experience creating cosmetic dentures. He first qualified in Dental Technology in Manchester in 1993 and has run a successful dental laboratory ever since.

Simon later completed advanced clinical training and gained a Diploma in Clinical Dental Technology, allowing him to register with the General Dental Council and work directly with patients. He assesses each person's smile, facial profile and speech to help achieve dentures that feel comfortable and look natural.

GDC registration number: 164114

Janine

Admin & Reception

Janine supports clients from the first enquiry onwards, helping to arrange appointments and ensuring you feel informed and comfortable throughout.
